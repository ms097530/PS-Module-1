Morning Magic Milkshake

A milkshake that's sure to wake you up and feeling ready to take on the day! Balancing out (?) the ice cream with some healthy choices and a bit of espresso to get you moving... you'll love it!

Ingredients

  • Milk (your choice of type)
  • Ice cream
  • Chocolate
  • Nuts
  • Spinach
  • Chia Seeds
  • Espresso

Method

  1. Pour in milk
  2. Add ice cream
  3. Mix in other ingredients
  4. Blend
